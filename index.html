Our story

Two hearts,
one passport.

We're Nived & Karthika — a newly married couple from Bangalore who decided the best way to start their forever was with a one-way ticket to Bali.

What started as a honeymoon became something bigger: a love for capturing the quiet magic of new places — the morning mist over rice terraces, the golden hour at a hidden café, the joy of getting beautifully lost together.

We create cinematic couple travel content focused on South India's most breathtaking destinations — and the stories that unfold along the way.

"Our Bali honeymoon was the beginning — Kodaikanal is just the next chapter."
